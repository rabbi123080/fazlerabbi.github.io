About Me

I am a Lecturer in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST), Cumilla, Bangladesh. My research interest includes broadly in Artificial Intelligence (AI), with a particular focus on the application of Machine Learning (ML) and Deep Learning (DL) techniques to various sectors. Prior to joining the faculty at BAIUST, I was an adjunct lecturer at the Bangladesh University of Textiles (BUTEX). I received my B.Sc. in CSE from Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET) in 2017.
